About L&L Games

L&L is a small game development group based out of Toronto, Canada. Our goal is to build fun and engaging games for children of all ages that teach patience, dexterity and strategy. We know kids love it because all of our games concepts and designs are done by kids! Each game is designed for both desktop (Chrome) and iOS.
